Kuidas mängida?

1. Enne mängu käivitamist veendu, et sul on paigaldatud "Bauhaus 93" ja "Comic Sans" font. 
   Nende puudumisel käivita mängu kaustas olevad failid "bauhaus" ja "comicsans" ning paigalda
   enda arvutisse need fondid.

2. Enne käivitamist veendu, et kõik failid on ühes ja samas kaustas.

3. Käivita "Sudoku.py", vajutades kaks (2) korda failile, mille nimi on mainitud lause esimeses osas.

4. Loodetavasti jõuad iseseisvalt mänguekraanile.

5. Numbri sisestamiseks vajuta tühjale kastile ning seejärel soovitavale numbrile all pool.

6. Naudi

How to play?

1. Before running the game make sure, that you "Bauhaus 93" and "Comic Sans" fonts are installed
   in your computer. If you do not have these fonts installed, run files "bauhaus" and "comicsans"
   To instal these fonts.

2. Before running the game, make sure, that all files are in the same folder.

3. Run "Sudoku.py" by clicking twice (2 times) on the file, which name was mentioned in the first part
   of this sentence.

4. Hopefully you will reach the playscreen by yourself.

5. To enter the desired number into sudoku grid, click on empty box and after that on the desired number below.

6. Enjoy!
